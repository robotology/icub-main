
========================================================================
extend

+ Takes a sequence of numbers, tries to find a pattern, and
  optionally guesses at the future of that sequence.

+ Copyright 2006 Paul Fitzpatrick
+ CopyPolicy: GNU Public License version 2

Libraries needed:

  boost; on debian: libboost-dev libboost-program-options-dev
                    libboost-regex-dev

========================================================================

Examples of usage:

$ ./extend 01010
pattern is (01)*

$ ./extend 01011
pattern is (0+1+)*

$ ./extend 01012 15
pattern is (012?)*
extended sequence by 15 from 01012: 012010120101012
